# Spirulina Hydrogel

## Recipe
- 40 mL distilled water
- 5 g spirulina biomass
- 2 g sodium alginate
- 2 g glycerol

## Objective
This formulation was used as a proof of concept for printing bio-derived materials.

## Notes
The objective was not to demonstrate viability, but successful extrusion and shape retention.

See attached photos and videos for results.
